# Bloom's Level: Analyze
## What it means
Breaking down information, identifying patterns, finding relationships.
## Question stems
- Why does...? What is the relationship between...? What evidence...? Compare the tradeoffs...
## Assessment types
- Analysis of scenarios
- Identifying assumptions and biases
- Debugging and root cause analysis
## Example
Q: Your model has 99% training accuracy but 60% test accuracy. Analyze what's happening.
A: The model is overfitting — memorizing training data rather than learning generalizable patterns.

# Bloom's Level: Remember
## What it means
Recalling facts, definitions, lists, and basic concepts from memory.
## Question stems
- What is...? Define... List... Name... Identify... State...
## Assessment types
- Multiple choice (recall facts)
- True/false
- Fill in the blank
- Matching
## Example
Q: What are the three types of machine learning?
A: Supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement learning

# Bloom's Level: Evaluate
## What it means
Making judgments, justifying decisions, critiquing approaches.
## Question stems
- Which approach is better and why? Justify... Critique... Defend... Assess...
## Assessment types
- Comparative analysis
- Defend a design decision
- Critique a solution
## Example
Q: A medical AI has 95% accuracy but 40% recall for cancer detection. Is this acceptable?
A: No — in medical diagnosis, recall (catching actual cases) is critical. Missing 60% of cancer cases is dangerous despite high overall accuracy.

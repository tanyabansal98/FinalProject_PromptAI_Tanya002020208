# Statistics Misconceptions
## Correlation implies causation
Ice cream sales and drowning deaths are correlated (both increase in summer). The cause is a third variable (temperature), not a causal link.
## p < 0.05 means 95% chance the result is true
The p-value is the probability of seeing this result IF the null hypothesis is true. It's not the probability that your hypothesis is correct.
## Averages tell the whole story
Mean salary can be skewed by outliers. Median is more robust. Always look at the distribution, not just the center.

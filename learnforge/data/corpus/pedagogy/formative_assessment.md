# Formative Assessment
## What it is
Low-stakes assessment during learning (not at the end) designed to identify gaps and guide instruction. Unlike summative assessment (final exam), formative assessment is for learning, not grading.
## Techniques
- Quick quizzes after each section
- Confidence-weighted responses (do you know this or are you guessing?)
- Misconception-targeted questions (distractors based on common errors)
- Self-assessment and reflection prompts
## Feedback principles
- Immediate feedback is better than delayed
- Explain WHY the answer is correct (not just right/wrong)
- Connect to the learning objective
- Suggest specific next steps

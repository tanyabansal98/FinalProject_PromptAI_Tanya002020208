# Scaffolding in Education
## What it is
Temporary support structures that help learners achieve tasks beyond their current ability. As competence grows, scaffolds are gradually removed.
## Types
- **Hints**: Progressively reveal information (mild → strong)
- **Worked examples**: Show step-by-step solutions before asking students to solve
- **Templates**: Provide structure for open-ended tasks
- **Prerequisites**: Ensure foundational knowledge before advancing
## Application
- In quizzes: provide hints before revealing answers
- In lessons: start with concrete examples before theory
- In curricula: enforce prerequisite modules
- Adapt difficulty based on performance (easier after failures, harder after success)
